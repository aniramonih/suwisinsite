Roman Atwood is an American YouTube personality, comedian, vlogger, content creator, and prankster who has gained immense popularity with his vlogs. His YouTube channel “RomanAtwoodVlogs” has accrued a fan base of more than 15 million subscribers while his other channel “RomanAtwood” has garnered more than 10 million subscribers. After Germán Garmendia, he became the 2nd YouTuber to receive two Diamond Play Buttons for his two channels.
