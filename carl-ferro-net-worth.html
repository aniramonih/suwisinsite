What is Carl Ferro's Net Worth?

Carl Ferro is an American businessman and entrepreneur who has a net worth of $10 million. Carl Ferro is the president and co-founder of the meal delivery service Sunfare. Ferro is best known for marrying actress Angie Everhart in December 2014. Carl's daughter JoAnna was the flower girl for the ceremony.

Ferro is a former trainer and nutrition counselor who is described as a health and fitness expert. He oversees daily operations of Sunfare including accounting and deliveries, employee relations, menu development, and hiring. Many of Sunfare's meals come from his secret family recipes. Ferro co-founded Sunfare with CEO John Stewart who he met at the University of Kansas.
