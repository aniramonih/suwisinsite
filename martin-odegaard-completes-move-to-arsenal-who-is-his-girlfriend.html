Learn About Martin Odegaard Parents

Martin Odegaard parents are Hans Erik Ødegaard and Lene Cecilie Ødegaard.

It is very obvious that the football gene runs in the Odegaard family. Martin’s father, Hans, is a former Norwegian football player. He rose to fame as a midfielder for the Strømsgodset (1993 to 2003).

Hans even served as the assistant manager for Strømsgodset from 2009 to 2015. Currently, he is working as the head coach for Sandefjord.

From an early age, Martin showed his enthusiasm for football. A native of Drammen, he spent his first five years in the Drammen Strong.

In 2009, Odegaard eventually joined the Strømsgodset under his father’s management. Despite training and playing with boys much older than his age, he apparently showed huge potential.
