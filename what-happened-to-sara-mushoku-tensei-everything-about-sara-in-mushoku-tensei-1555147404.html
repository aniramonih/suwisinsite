Mushoku Tensei Wiki

Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation, is a Japanese anime television series inspired by the equally enchanting light novel series of the same name. The story unfolds around a man burdened by a life filled with hopelessness and unemployment, his existence steeped in sadness and seclusion.

However, his tale takes an unexpected twist as he meets his demise, only to be reborn in a wondrous fantasy realm, retaining his memories from his previous life. With a newfound determination to seize this second chance, he embraces his fresh start under the name Rudeus Greyrat, vowing to relish his new life without any lingering regrets.

In the exciting announcement that stirred fans' curiosity, it was revealed on March 6, 2022, that a second season of Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation had received the green light. This season, helmed by director Hiroki Hirano, promises to captivate audiences once again as it brings to life the events chronicled in volumes seven and beyond of the light novel series. 

With scripts supervised by Toshiya Ono and character designs masterfully crafted by Sanae Shimada, the second season of Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation will be a visual feast for fans of the series. Comprising a total of 25 episodes, the first part of the season is set to premiere on July 3, 2023, promising to whisk viewers away into a world brimming with wonder and adventure.

Mushoku Tensei Anime Plot

Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation" follows the transformative journey of Rudeus Greyrat, a 34-year-old man who, after leading a regretful and unfulfilled life, meets his demise. However, he is given a second chance when he is reincarnated in a magical world filled with fantastical beings and enchanting adventures.

Embracing this new lease on life, Rudeus, now in the body of a young boy, resolves to make the most of his reincarnation and rectify the mistakes of his past. With the knowledge and wisdom he gained from his previous life, he sets out on a path of personal growth and redemption.

As Rudeus navigates this fantasy realm, he encounters a diverse cast of characters who shape his journey.
