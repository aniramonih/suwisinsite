My Unorthodox Life: Who is Julia Haarts ex husband? Yosef Hendler stars in Netflix series

Publish date: 2024-08-06
Spread the love

Netflix’s gripping series, “My Unorthodox Life,” introduces us to Julia Haart, a remarkable entrepreneur who broke free from her ultra-orthodox Jewish background. While Julia takes center stage, her ex-husband, Yosef Hendler, also plays a significant role in her life. In this article, we dive into the life of Yosef Hendler and his journey through the show.

Julia Haart’s Ex-Husband: Meet Yosef Hendler

Julia Haart and Yosef Hendler’s union brought four children into the world: Batsheva, Shlomo, Miriam, and Aron. Yosef, a pivotal figure in the 2021 Netflix series, first appears in episode 2 when the subject of Miriam officially changing her surname from Hendler to Haart arises.

At 55 years old, Yosef Hendler displays understanding and support on the Netflix show when Miriam expresses her desire to alter her name. His heartfelt response reflects his love for his daughter, stating, “Everyone has free will, so for you to choose a name that gives you a better feeling of where you are today, I’ll respect that. That’s fine. I love you. You’re my daughter.”

The Split: When Did Yosef and Julia Part Ways?

Yosef Hendler and Julia Haart entered an arranged marriage over 30 years ago when Julia was merely 19 years old, while Yosef was 2Their journey took a significant turn around a decade ago when Julia decided to leave the ultra-orthodox community. In the series’ first episode, Julia candidly expressed her feelings, stating, “Marriage was prison, I hated being married.”

Julia’s departure marked the end of her first marriage. In 2019, she embarked on a new chapter in her life, marrying entrepreneur Silvio Scaglia. Their love story continues to flourish to this day.

Yosef Hendler’s New Beginning

“My Unorthodox Life” unveils a surprising development in Yosef Hendler’s life. Following his separation from Julia, Yosef ventured into the world of dating. During the show, he shares that he had been in a relationship with Aliza Schulhof for three months at the time of filming. In a heartwarming twist, it is revealed that Yosef and Aliza are now engaged, as confirmed by Batsheva’s TikTok.

Notably, Aliza Schulhof’s connection to Julia adds an intriguing layer to the story. Aliza’s ex-husband was Julia’s orthodontist, a revelation that Julia herself found astonishing. Aliza, a talented party planner, can be found on Instagram under the handle @perfectplansbylisa, where she boasts over 6,000 followers.

In the world of “My Unorthodox Life,” Yosef Hendler’s journey showcases resilience, adaptability, and the enduring capacity to find love once more.

Conclusion

While “My Unorthodox Life” primarily focuses on Julia Haart’s transformative journey, Yosef Hendler’s presence adds depth to the narrative. His understanding and support for his daughter’s choices, as well as his own path to love and engagement, offer viewers a glimpse into the intricate tapestry of life beyond tradition and convention.
