Who is Jack Gleeson?

Jack Gleeson is an Irish actor who started acting in 2002. He is best known as Joffrey Baratheon on the popular television series Game of Thrones from 2011 until 2014.

There is no information on his parents. He grew up with his sisters Rachel and Emma who are also actresses in Ireland.
