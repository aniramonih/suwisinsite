What Happened To Chuck Goudies Wife, Teri Goudie? Twitter Floods With Tribute Post

Publish date: 2024-08-20

On the 1st of June 2022, Teri Goudie, wife of American television journalist, Chuck Goudie, passed away.

Goudie passed away a few days before her 65th birthday. Her death was announced by her daughter, Grace Goudie, in an Instagram post.

Following the news of Goudie’s death, there have been many sympathetic outpourings of sorrow. Twitter is flooded with tribute posts remembering and honoring Goudie.

Goudie’s husband, Chuck, also honored his wife by reprinting a column he’d written in her memory 15 years ago in NewsFinale. Following the news of her death, her obituary mentioned that donations in her name would be welcome for the Chicago Marathon Lymphoma and Leukemia Team in Training.

Teri Goudie is survived not only by her husband, Chuck, and her five children but also by her community in Chicago, who mourn her passing.

Who Is Chuck Goodie’s Wife, Teri Goudie?

Teri Goudie was the founder and Ceo of Goudie Media Services.

Goudie Media Services is a media training company based in Chicago, Illinois, that provides professional training and coaching in presentation, leadership development, keynote performances, crisis management, and core story creation, among other things.

The company has worked with high-profile companies such as Google, McDonald’s, Abbott, United, etc. Teri Goudie developed her method, called the Goudie method, a five-step method used in presentations, calls, interviews, and social media interaction.

Goudie was supported in her business by her daughter Caylen Bufalino. Caylen is a partner in Goudie Media Services and co-founded The Headliners, another media training company, with her mother.

Just like her husband, Goudie also has a history in television journalism. From 1979 to shortly before her death, Goodie was a producer for ABC News. Before this, she worked as a broadcast reporter for several years.

Goudie was also a print journalist and wrote her newspaper column for 15 years.

What Happened To Chuck Goudie’s Wife, Teri Goudie? Cause of Death

The details of Teri Goudie’s cause of death have not been revealed to the public.

Her obituary mentioned that she passed away peacefully, and none of the tributes to her memory stated anything about how she passed away. However, Goudie was fighting a lengthy battle with lymphoma before her passing.

Her battle with lymphoma was the lead cause behind her health issues during the last years of her life, and it’s looking like it’s what caused her death as well. Still, lymphoma cannot be verified yet as Goudie’s cause of death; neither the family nor her obituary has stated anything along the matter.

Teri Goudie: Children And Married Life

Teri Gouldie had five children with her husband, Chuck Goudie.

Out of Gouldie’s five children, Brittanny Goudie is following in her father’s footsteps as she worked on The Oprah Winfrey Show during the beginning of her broadcast career. Currently, she manages music at the Nashville-based Starstruck Entertainment and produces the SiriusXM The Highway morning show with Storm Warren.

Another of her children is Caylen Bufalino, who worked with her mother as a partner in Goudie Media Services and co-founded The Headliners with Teri Goudie. Bufalino was often touted as Teri’s protege and the next in line for Goudie’s various media training companies.

However, the most popular of Goudie’s children is undoubtedly Grace Goudie. Grace is an up-and-coming chef who recently debuted her show, Chopped, on the food network.

All the Gouldie children paid tribute to their mother on social media, and Grace’s post first broke the news of her passing.

By all accounts, Teri and her husband Chuck were deeply in love and happily married.
