Who are Rihannas Siblings? Meet Her Brothers and Sisters

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Rihanna has five siblings. The 35-year-old world-famous Barbadian singer, businesswoman, and actress, has two full-blooded brothers – Rorrey Fenty and Rajad Fenty, two half-sisters – Kandy Fenty and Samantha Fenty, and another half-brother – Jamie Fenty.

Rihanna is known for being very close to her siblings, and she has made a habit of regularly spending time with them. Her bond with her siblings is strong, and she values their relationship greatly. Given her worldwide fame, it is not surprising that her family members have become well-known figures too. This is largely because Rihanna’s fame has brought a lot of attention to her family.

How Many Siblings Does Rihanna Have?

Rihanna has five siblings. She has two brothers, Rorrey Fenty and Rajad Fenty, from the same parents, and they grew up together. For the record, Rihanna was born on February 20, 1988, in Saint Michael, Barbados, as the eldest of three children born to her parents, Ronald Fenty and Monica Braithwaite.

Unfortunately, her parent’s marriage did not endure, and they eventually got divorced without reconciling. Despite this, Rihanna was raised in Barbados along with her two brothers, Rorrey and Rajad Fenty, and developed strong bonds with them both. She often mentions her brothers in interviews and shows her love and support for them through various social media posts.

In addition to her two full brothers, Rihanna also has three half-siblings – two half-sisters, Kandy and Samantha, and one half-brother, Jamie. Reports suggest that she also has a close relationship with her half-siblings and often meets with them. This highlights her love and affection for her family members, regardless of their shared bloodlines.

It is on record that at the age of 16, Rihanna boldly relocated to the United States to pursue her dream of becoming a musician. Her talent, hard work, and dedication paid off, and she has since become one of the most successful and recognizable figures in the entertainment industry. Despite her busy schedule, she has maintained close ties with her family, particularly her two younger full brothers, Rorrey and Rajad.

The three siblings are known to be very close, and they often spend quality time together, reminding us that despite her fame and success, Rihanna remains down-to-earth and grounded.
