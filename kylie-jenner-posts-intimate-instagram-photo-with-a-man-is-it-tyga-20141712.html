Kylie Jenner Posts Intimate Instagram Photo With a Man: Is It Tyga?

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Scandalous! Kylie Jenner cozied up to a man in an intimate, albeit mysterious, new photo on Instagram.

The reality star, 17, shared the photo in the early hours of Wednesday, Dec. 17, without a caption. 

In the tight shot, she is shown snuggling up to a man, holding his gold chain-adorned neck with her manicured hand. Jenner’s dark locks cover one of her eyes, while the other is shown with a dramatic smoky eye and long lashes. The teen star also offers a peek at her now-famous, talked-about plump lips. 

The man whom she is holding onto appears to be her current romantic interest, rapper Tyga. Just above the chain necklace, a sliver of a black tattoo can be seen on the neck.
