What is Mynique Smith's Net Worth?

Mynique Smith is an American personal trainer and reality television personality who has a net worth of $500 thousand. Mynique Smith is married to former NFL player Chuck Smith. Chuck and Mynique have three children together and live in Atlanta, Georgia. Mynique Smith bills herself as a personal trainer to the stars and may soon be a certifiable star herself.

In August 2013, rumors began swirling that Mynique is set to become the newest cast member of Bravo's reality series The Real Housewives of Miami. She was even spotted filming some scenes with the ladies in Savannah, GA. Mynique would potentially be a very good choice as a cast member considering she is already very good friends with perennial favorite housewife Nene Leakes. So far the announcement has not been made official by the people at Bravo but her husband Chuck has expressed his support in the endeavor if it ends up happening.
