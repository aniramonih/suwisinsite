What is Kelly Sheridan's Net Worth?

Kelly Sheridan is a Canadian voice actress who has a net worth of $2 million. Kelly Sheridan was born in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada in May 1977. She is best known for providing the voice of Barbie in her film series from 2001 to 2005. Sheridan has also provided voice work for many cartoons and English dubs of Japanese anime. She voiced the role of Ukyo Kuonji on the TV series Ranma ½: Netto-hen from 1990 to 1991. Sheridan voiced the role of Melody on the series My Little Pony Tales in 1992.

In 1994 she provided the voice of Brynne on the television series Conan and the Young Warriors. Sheridan voiced the role of Tsukushi Makino on the TV series Boys Over Flowers in 1996. The same year she voiced the role of Hitomi Kanzaki on the series Escaflowne. She voiced Yuki Saiko on the television series Silent Mobius in 1998. Sheridan has also voiced roles for many other TV series including Dragon Ball Z, X-Men: Evolution, Inuyasha, Martin Mystery, Nana, Class of the Titans, Care Bears: Adventures in Care-A-Lot, Mobile Suit Gundam 00, InuYasha: The Final Act, Hero: 108, Rekkit Rabbit, LoliRock, My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic, and more. Sheridan won a Behind the Voice Actors Award in 2016 for Barbie in Princess Power.
