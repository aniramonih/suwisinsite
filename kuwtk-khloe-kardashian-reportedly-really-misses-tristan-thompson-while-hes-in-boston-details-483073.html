KUWTK: Khloe Kardashian Reportedly Really Misses Tristan Thompson While Hes In Boston Details

Publish date: 2024-08-23

According to an insider, Khloe Kardashian really misses her baby daddy, Tristan Thompson, a lot these days and would love nothing more than to spend more time with the basketball star while he’s away in Boston. As fans know, the reality TV celeb has been home in L.A. with their daughter, True while he plays for the Boston Celtics.

Obviously, having him a six hour plane ride away is not ideal but Khloe has been trying her best to be supportive.

The source tells HollywoodLife that ‘Khloe has been wanting to spend more time with Tristan lately but he has not had the time to be with her or True. It has been harder to co-parent because of the NBA season and they have all had little time together.’

And the sheer distance is not the only obstacle in their way.

You might be aware that the NBA has also imposed pretty strict quarantine rules for its players because of the pandemic.

During the newest episode of KUWTK, Khloe stressed that she and Tristan are not back ‘together romantically.’

Despite this, they still have a very close relationship while co-parenting their child.
