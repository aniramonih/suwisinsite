Michael Caine

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Caine in 2007, photo by prphotos

Birth Name: Maurice Joseph Micklewhite

Place of Birth: St. Olave Bermondsey, London, England, U.K.

Date of Birth: 14 March, 1933

Ethnicity: English, Irish

Michael Caine is a British actor and author. He has twice won the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor, for Hannah and Her Sisters (1986) and The Cider House Rules (1999).

He is the son of Ellen Frances Marie/Maria (Burchell) and Maurice Joseph Micklewhite. His brother was actor Stanley Caine. Michael is married to Indian-Guyanese-British actress and model Shakira Caine. He has a daughter with his former wife, actress Patricia Haines; and a daughter with Shakira.

Michael has English and Irish ancestry. On Inside the Actors Studio (2000), Michael stated that his father had Romani ancestry. It is not clear if this Romani ancestry has been verified/documented. He has said this on other occasions.

Michael’s surname was spelled Mickelwait several generations back.

Michael’s paternal grandfather was Joseph Henry Micklewhite (the son of Joseph Henry Micklewhite and Catherine Martha Turner). Michael’s grandfather Joseph was born in St. Saviour Southwark, London, England. Michael’s great-grandfather Joseph was the son of George Gilham Scott Mickelthwaite and Emma Russell.

Michael’s paternal grandmother was Johanna/Hannah/Hanna Regan (the daughter of Maurice Regan and Ellen Regan). Johanna was born in St. Saviour Southwark, London, England. Michael’s great-grandfather Maurice was the son of Irish parents, Timothy Regan and Hannah Drummony. Ellen was the daughter of Dennis Regan and Mary, who were from Cork, Ireland.

Michael’s maternal grandfather was John Burchell (the son of John Burchell and Maria Smith). Michael’s grandfather John was born in Southwark, London, England. Michael’s great-grandfather John was the son of James Burchell and Jane, who were from Cork, Ireland. Maria was the daughter of James Smith and Maria.

Michael’s maternal grandmother was named Phoebe Stokes (the daughter of Charles William Stokes and Lydia Jane). Phoebe was born on Bermuda, to English parents. Charles was born in Portsmouth, Hampshire.

Michael attended the Hackney Downs Grocers’ School, which had many Jewish students. While there, he learned to speak Yiddish.
