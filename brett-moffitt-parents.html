Brett Moffitt Parents Dick And Becky Moffitt- Family Tree

Publish date: 2024-08-11

Brett Moffitt is an American professional stock car racing driver. Born the youngest to his parents, Brett got everything he wanted as a kid and received all the love and affection from his elders.

At 10, Brett began his racing career as a kart racer around 2002. As he kept improving and eventually decided to have a sports career, his parents homeschooled him during the last two years of high school.

Moffitt won the 2007 Harris Clash in the IMCA Sport Modified division.

He finally made his NASCAR touring series debut in 2009, driving in the Camping World East Series for a team owned by former racer Andy Santerre.

Even though Brett is a successful truck series racing driver with twelve wins, he has not been much fortunate in the NASCAR Xfinity Series.

His best achievement in Xfinity Series to date is finishing at 21st position, consecutively in 2021 and 2022.

Moffitt drives the No. 25 Ford Mustang for the AM Racing in the Xfinity Series. He signed up with the team for full-time racing in 2023.

Brett Moffitt Parents: Who Are Dick And Becky Moffitt?

Moffitt was born on August 7, 1992, to his parents, Dick and Becky Crouse Moffitt, in Grimes, Iowa, United States. Besides, he has a sister, Allison Moffitt, nicknamed Alle.

Alle attended the University of Michigan, graduating with a bachelor’s degree in Global Public Health in 2014. She is already married and lives in Georgia, Atlanta.

Unlike other athletes, Brett was not a sports guy as a kid and did not go outdoors to play with his friends.

His father, Dick, in the hope that Brett would have fun, once asked him to participate in a kart racing competition when Brett was just ten years old.

Before that, he had not even watched an auto race. And Surprisingly, Brett agreed to it, and the rest is history. 

His dad was a partner in the Destiny Homes home-building company based in Waukee, Iowa. After his son began showing interest in racing, Dick gave all the time to Brett in order to work on his practices.

His dad has been by his side since the beginning and has attended almost all the races he has participated in his career to date. Both son and dad accept that the sport strengthened their bonding.

Unfortunately, there is not much information about his mother, Becky, except that she is a housewife. 

Brett Is A Married Man

The NASCAR Xfinity Series driver is married to his longtime girlfriend, Stephanie Moffitt. Based on his Instagram posts, the two probably started dating in 2018.

Stephanie is originally from North Carolina and celebrates her birthday in July. Besides, nothing much about her life before she met Brett is known.

After being together for about three years, Brett tied the knot with Stephanie on April 4, 2021, with the blessings from their close ones in a Christian ceremony.

On July 25, 2021, the pair announced they were expecting a baby. Stephanie eventually gave birth to their first child, a son, Cooper Ray Moffit, in February 2022.

Currently, the couple resides in North Carolina with their son and three dogs.
