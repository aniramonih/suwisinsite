Roman Atwood's Family: Kids, Wife, Siblings, Mother, Father

Publish date: 2024-08-20
The Family of Youtube Star Roman Atwood

Roman Atwood is among the most popular vloggers and pranksters in the world, who gained two diamond buttons from Youtube. His videos earned more than 4 billion views and raised the man to the list of 100 most subscribed personalities on Youtube.   

Date of Birth: May 28, 1983

Zodiac Sign: Gemini

Initially, Roman worked at Atwood Rope, a factory owned by his family. Later, he became a producer of The Nerd Nerd DVD series and took part in various projects, related to film and advertising industry. In 2010, Atwood decided to create his own Youtube channel to share funny videos there and since then, his popularity grows with every year. Additionally, the entertainer owns a merchandise line, Smile More, dealing with clothes, accessories and toys.

But today, we aren’t going to tell about Roman’s achievements, but are eager to share some information about his close relatives.    

Roman Atwood father

Curtis Dale Atwood II (father)

Date of Birth: August 16, 1959

Zodiac Sign: Leo

Curtis is the owner of Atwood Rope MFG, which he founded in 1985. Roman is very proud of his dad and shares that he would be happy to be at least half as great as his father. In 2016, Curtis got a cool present from his son on his birthday, a red Chevrolet Corvette (the car, he had dreamt about for a long time).

Roman Atwood mother

Susan Anne Christman (mother)

Roman with mom and dad

Date of Birth: October 13, 1960

Date of Death: May 27-29, 2019 (age 58)

Zodiac Sign: Libra

Currently residing in Ohio with her husband, Susan helps to run their family rope-manufacturing factory. Besides Roman, she has one more son, Dale.

Updated. Susan fell off from scooter and hit her head while riding around with Roman’s nephew. She died immediately being only 58 years old. RIP


Roman Atwood siblings: brother

Curtis Dale Atwood III (brother)

Date of Birth: May 7, 1985

Zodiac Sign: Taurus

Dale is the Youtuber’s younger brother. Though he didn’t plan to become a prankster, the example of his sibling inspired him to try his hand at vlogging. His Youtube channel was created in 2015 and is pretty popular now. Dale is a husband to Jessica Atwood and is a dad of two boys. Besides, he adores traveling with his family.

Roman Atwood ex-wife

Shanna Janette Riley (ex-spouse)

Duration of Marriage: 2001-2008 (7 years)

Date of Birth: February 21, 1983

Zodiac Sign: Pisces

Roman got romantically involved with Shanna in 1999 and espoused her on November 17, 2001. However, they separated in 2008 because of Shanna’s infidelity. She is rumored to cheat on her husband with their mutual friend and a representative of Atwood’s production team. The ex-spouses seem to be in bad relations.

Roman Atwood wife

Brittney Danielle Atwood (nee Smith) (current wife)

Duration of Marriage: since 2018 till present

Date of Birth: February 16, 1991

Zodiac Sign: Aquarius

Brittney worked as a dentist’s assistant, but today she runs Smile More together with her husband and makes appearances in his videos. She is also pretty popular on Instagram and has about 3 million of followers. The future spouses got acquainted in 2008 at the nuptials of Roman’s friend and Brittney’s sister. Before marriage she gave birth to their son Kane. In April 2018, the celebrity proposed to his girlfriend and espoused her on July 27 in Hawaii. Brittney is a mom of two, son Kane and daughter Cora. By the way, Brittney has two tattoos on her fingers, dedicated to her spouse: letter R and a heart, which symbolizes her love to Roman.

Roman Atwood children: 2 sons and a daughter

Noah Vaughn Atwood (son with Shanna Riley)

Date of Birth: October 18, 2004

Zodiac Sign: Libra

Noah is the eldest son of the famed vlogger, who lives with Roman. He meets his biological mom rather seldom. As Noah was often featured in his dad’s videos, Shanna accused Roman of child labor exploitation, but the court was on Atwood’s side and granted him the full custody over Noah. In addition to being a frequent guest at his dad’s pranks, Noah helps to advertise Atwood’s brand Smile More.

Kane Alexander Atwood (son with Brittney Atwood)

Date of Birth: October 23, 2011

Zodiac Sign: Scorpio

Along with his elder half-brother Noah, Kane often appears on his dad’s videos and advertises his business ventures. He was first engaged into his dad’s pranks when he was only 5 months old. In one of his pranks, Roman tried to make his wife believe that he killed Kane while, the next time, he assured Brittney that the boy’s four-wheeler exploded. However, don’t worry, Kane wasn’t hurt.

Cora Atwood (daughter with Brittney Atwood)

Date of Birth: July 16, 2017

Zodiac Sign: Cancer

Cora is Roman’s little princess. Her birth was announced on her dad’s Instagram. Moreover, Atwood dedicated a vlog to her birth, which earned more than seven million views within seven days after its release. Cora’s photos are often shared by both parents on their social media.
