What is Penny Johnson Jerald's Net Worth?

Penny Johnson Jerald is an American actress who has a net worth of $3 million. Penny Johnson Jerald was born in Baltimore, Maryland in March 1961. Penny trained at the Juilliard School. Her debut acting role came in the TV series American Playhouse in 1983. Her film debuts came in the movies Swing Shift and The Hills Have Eyes Part II in 1984. From 1984 to 1986 Jerald starred as Vivian Conway in the television series The Paper Chase. In 1989 she starred as Virginia Harper in the TV series Homeroom.

Penny starred as Beverly Barnes in The Larry Sanders Show from 1992 to 1998. From 1998 to 1999 she starred as Nurse Practitioner Lynette Evans in the series ER. From 1995 to 1999 she had a recurring role in the TV series Star Trek: Deep Space Nine. From 2001 to 2004 she starred as Sherry Palmer in the series 24. She starred as Dean Etwood in the series October Road from 2007 to 2008 and has starred as Victoria Gates in the series Castle since 2011. She has been nominated for a Screen Actors Guild Award and an Image Award.
