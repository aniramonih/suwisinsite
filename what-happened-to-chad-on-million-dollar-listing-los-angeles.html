What happened to Chad on Million Dollar Listing Los Angeles?

Publish date: 2024-08-17

Chad Rogers starred on the first three seasons of Million Dollar Listing Los Angeles, which aired on Bravo between 2006 and 2009.

However, when Chad decided to leave the show behind, he was replaced by Josh Altman who remains a recurring character on the show.

When Chad was on the show, he was just 28 years old, but he was doing million-dollar deals and competing with the best in the Los Angeles real estate industry.

Just because he left the Bravo show behind, it doesn’t mean he left the real estate industry behind.

In fact, he often talks about real estate on his Instagram profile, including sharing photos of his listings. As for how he’s doing financially, he posted a photo from his supposed Malibu home, which includes a pool and a tennis court. It looks pretty fabulous!

According to Hilton & Hyland, Chad pulled in $82 million in sales in 2015 and given his commission, he walked away with a couple of million in profit that year alone. Given he’s been in real estate since his 20s, Chad Rogers’ net worth is $10 million.

As for his romance life, he appears to be linked to personal trainer Jenna Willis.

Her Instagram account reveals that she worked with Lala Kent get fit and she seems to be close with both Lala and Randall Emmett.

While we couldn’t confirm whether Chad and Jenna are married or just dating, they are serious as they spent New Year’s Eve together.

Chad is also serious about his fitness. He’s now 41 years old and he’s taking his health and fitness seriously. You can easily find a shirtless photo of him on Instagram.

He’s also the founder of Raw Raw Land Chicken Jerky, something he proudly promoted on Twitter. This is a rather new business venture for him, but he’s already landed himself some shelf space at Pacific Coast Greens.

Chad Rogers made headlines in the spring of 2018 when he released a song called Maliboo. He even did a music video for it.

It’s clear to see that Chad isn’t slowing down, even though he’s no longer in the public eye with Million Dollar Listing Los Angeles.

Million Dollar Listing Los Angeles airs Thursdays at 9/8c on Bravo.
