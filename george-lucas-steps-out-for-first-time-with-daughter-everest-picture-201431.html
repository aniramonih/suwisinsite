George Lucas Steps Out For First Time With Daughter Everest: Picture

Publish date: 2024-08-09

His little angel! George Lucas and his wife Mellody Hobson stepped out with their daughter Everest on New Year's Day, giving the world a sneak peek at their new addition. The proud parents were photographed enjoying a boat ride in St. Barts while doting on their sweet baby girl.

During the holiday, Lucas, 69, and Hobson, 44, were spotted holding up their 5-month-old little tot while trying to put a life jacket on her. She also donned a white onesie and adorable pink bow in her hair.
