Gadars director Anil Sharma talks about the sequel of the film

Publish date: 2024-08-15


Gadar was released in 2001 and starred Sunny Deol and Ameesha Patel. The film became a huge box-office success and had people in huge groups arriving to watch it in theatres. Gadar was directed by Anil Sharma. The director is currently busy chalking out a plan for the sequel of Apne starring Dharmendra, Sunny Deol, Bobby Deol and Karan Deol. However, as Gadar completes 20 years since its release tomorrow, ahead of the D-Day, the director caught up with a leading entertainment portal and spoke about the sequel of Gadar.

 


He said when he gets a script which has a combination of drama and realism, he will definitely think of making it. He also added that his son, who played Sunny Deol’s son in the film, Jeetu, is all grown up now and he might too be seen in the sequel. Well, here’s hoping something gets finalized soon.
