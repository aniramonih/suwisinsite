What is Marty York's Net Worth?

Marty York is an American actor who has a net worth of $200 thousand. Marty York was born in Auburn, California in August 1980. He is best known for his first role starring as Alan "Yeah-Yeah" McClennan in the 1993 movie "The Sandlot." York also appeared in episodes of the TV series "Saved by the Bell: The College Years," "Boy Meets World," "Wings," "Duckman: Private Dick/Family Man" (voice), "Sliders," "Hey Arnold!" (voice), "The Eric Andre Show," and "SMILF. "

In 2010 he starred in a short called "The Trainer." In 2011 he appeared in the film "The Brothers Sinclair."

Marty York won a Young Artist Award in 1994 for Outstanding Youth Ensemble in a Motion Picture for The Sandlot. In addition to pursuing an acting career he also attended school for firefighting.
