Bryan Clark Pays Tribute To Wrestling Legend In The Spirit Of Halloween

Publish date: 2024-08-12

It is the spooky season of the year, ladies and gentlemen. In the world of professional wrestling, it is the time of the year for Halloween Havoc. The pay-per-view was one of the most popular WCW events. Now, it lives on as an NXT special event.

One wrestling legend, Bryan Clark, wanted to share some fond memories in light of Halloween.

Bryan Clark was part of the popular WCW faction, Kronik. His tag team partner was Brian Adams. The two wrestlers had an incredible run in the wrestling business. The duo managed to capture the WCW World Tag Team Championships twice during their run.

Bryan Clark has also portrayed different characters throughout his professional wrestling career. One of his infamous gimmicks was Adam Bomb. Another one of his gimmicks was Wrath. He had a bunch of memorable matches while he was portraying that gimmick.

Recently, the former WCW wrestler took to Twitter to reflect on one of the biggest matches of his wrestling career. It was at Halloween Havoc 1998 at the MGM Grand Arena in Las Vegas, Nevada. It was a match against the wrestling giant King Haku.

“In the Spirit of Halloween and Halloween Havoc at MGM Grand Las Vegas. 25 years since I got one of the Baddest Men to ever lace a pair of Boots UP for Meltdown. Meng The King Haku was The Original “Monster Among Men”. So Much #Respect and thankful to have worked with such a PRO? I just got Lucky because we were in Las Vegas?️ Thank you my friend The King Haku Bryan,” Bryan Clark tweeted.

King Haku used to be part of a tag team along with the Eighth Wonder of the World, Andre The Giant. The duo managed to capture the WWF Tag Team Championship once.
