Film Editor, Molly Evangeline Goodman- Meet Single Daughter Of John Goodman, and Anna Beth Goodman

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Molly Evangeline Goodman is a TV personality who is better known as the daughter of American Emmy award-winning actor, John Goodman. Her mother's name is Anna Beth Goodman, a businesswoman who has launched a clothing line named Pippen Lane.

Besides, Molly is a production assistant and one of her flourishing works is the TV series New Girl on Fox Broadcasting Company. It aired between 2011 and 2018. Stick with us to find out more about her. 

Is Molly Goodman Married?

No, Molly Evangeline Goodman is still an unmarried woman. Despite being the star's daughter, she doesn’t want to live her life in the limelight.

Most of the time, Evangeline Goodman has always been tight-lipped when it comes to disclosing her relationship status to the public. As a result, her boyfriend's or husband's identity is also kept under wraps. 

Now, she seems to be a single woman who is living her life without dating rumors. Further, she's rarely seen with her dad, John in the public eye. Back in 2011, the daughter and father duo attended The Artist Special Screening presented by Audi.

Parents John and Anna Three Decades Of Married Life

Molly's  dad, John, and mom, Anna, who goes by Annabeth, are in a strong marital relationship for over three decades. In 2020, they celebrated their 31st marriage anniversary.

The lovely couple first met at a Halloween party in 1987 in New Orleans. During that period, Goodman was filming the 1988's film Everybody's All-American. Whereas Anna was studying at the University of New Orleans as a fine-arts student at the time. 

Despite having an age gap of 16 between the couple, John and Anna still felt a genuine bond and began dating. Finally, the lovebirds exchanged vows on October 27, 1989, in a private ceremony in New Orleans.

The ex-couple Bruce Willis and Demi Moore were among the wedding special guests. One year later, their daughter, Molly Evangeline Goodman was born in 1990. Now, the entire family resides in New Orleans' Garden District.

Molly Goodman's Estimated Net Worth

Molly Goodman enjoys an estimated worth of $500 thousand. As a film editor, she has worked on several projects such as The Last Tycoon, Traitor Knight, Different Flowers, etc. Even she served on her dad's film A Love Saga as a producer and editor.

Since 2018, Evangeline serves at ABC Television as a set dresser. As per sources, set dressers annually make approximately $75,500. However, her dad, John Goodman, a theatre, film, and TV actor enjoys $45 million worth.

With a career spanning nearly four decades, Goodman is best known for his role as Dan Conner on the show Roseanne and his roles in many Coen Brothers films. To date, he has acted in over 160 films and TV series including Patriots Day, Bringing out the Dead, Alpha House, Damages, etc.

The main roles in the TV series The Conners as Dan Conner and The Righteous Gemstones as Eli Gemstone were also played by John.

Father John's Real Estates

The frequent host of Saturday Night Live, Goodman resides in a $4.6 million home in the Pacific Palisade. It has five bedrooms.

Previously, he paid $1.8 million in 2005 to Joseph Merrick Jones for the 4,900 square feet home located in New Orleans. Further, he sold a Hollywood Hills residence for $560,500, Encino, California for $1.98 million, and bought an $875,000 home in Calabasas.

Bio and Early Life

Molly Evangeline Goodman was born on August 31, 1990, in St. Louis, Missouri, United States. She is the granddaughter of Virginia Roos and Leslie F. Goodman.

Her uncle is Leslie Goodman and Aunty is Elisabeth Goodman. As per her education, she earned her Bachelor’s degree from the University of Southern California in Film and Television Production.
