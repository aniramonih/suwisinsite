Boosie Badazz Wants To Hire Mermaids For His New Pool

Publish date: 2024-08-25

Boosie Badazz is looking to employ a few women for the summer – and all they have to do is hang out in his pool in a mermaid costume.

The Baton Rouge native Tweeted his job listing of sorts on Sunday (February 26). Revealing he’d be calling the pool area “Las Vegas, Georgia,” Boosie requested three women for the mermaid position and an additional 10 women to be “show girls.”
