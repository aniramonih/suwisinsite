Acrimony 2 Release Date: When Will the Sequel Hit the Screens?

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Taraji P. Henson and Tyler Perry’s psychological thriller Acrimony from 2018 was a big hit with fans of the genre. The film portrayed the narrative of Melinda, a devoted wife who felt betrayed by her husband’s success following their divorce, and how her resentment escalated into a violent altercation.

The film concluded on a cliffhanger, leaving viewers wondering what happened to the characters next. Will there be a sequel to acrimony? If so, when will it be available? Here’s all we know about Acrimony 2 so far.

Acrimony 2 Release Date

There has been no formal announcement of the release date for Acrimony 2 as of January 16, 2024. However, based on Tyler Perry’s and Studio’s production timetable, several sources estimate that the sequel will be released in late 2023 or early 2024.

The first film, which was shot in eight days in October 2017, was released on March 30, 2018. If the sequel follows a similar timeframe, it may be released in theatres approximately a year after filming begins. However, this is not confirmed, and fans must wait for an official confirmation from Tyler Perry or Lionsgate, the film’s distributor.

Acrimony 2 Cast

The cast of Acrimony 2 has yet to be announced; however, it is expected that the principal performers from the previous film will repeat their roles. The sequel may have some of the same actors from the previous film, such as:

  • Taraji P. Henson as Melinda Moore Gayle
  • Lyriq Bent as Robert Gayle
  • Crystle Stewart as Diana Wells
  • Ptosha Storey as Brenda Moore
  • Jazmyn Simon as June Moore
  • Ajiona Alexus as Young Melinda Moore Gayle
  • Antonio Madison as Young Robert Gayle

Depending on the content and direction of the film, the sequel may also feature some new characters.

Acrimony 2 Plot

The plot of Acrimony 2 has not been officially revealed, but there are various fan hypotheses and predictions based on the previous film’s ending. The first film concluded with Melinda falling off a boat and drowning, presumably after attempting to murder Robert and Diana, who had escaped on a lifeboat.

Some followers believe Melinda survived the fall and will return to exact revenge on Robert and Diana. Others believe Melinda died but her soul will haunt Robert and Diana, or that she has a twin sister who will take her place.

Some fans are hoping that the sequel will dive more into Robert and Diana’s relationship and whether or not they will face any difficulties or hurdles in their marriage.

Acrimony 2 Expectations

Acrimony 2, like the original film, is set to be a thrilling and emotional experience. Fans are curious to watch how Melinda and Robert’s story unfolds and whether there is any hope for repentance or reconciliation.

The film might also delve more into the themes of duplicity, resentment, and the devastating power of bitterness, as well as reveal the consequences of the characters’ actions.

Acrimony 2 may also address some of the questions and objections presented by the previous film, such as the portrayal of mental illness, the role of gender and ethnicity, and the plot’s legal and ethical issues.
