Who Is Julian Bass? He Went Viral On Tik Tok With VFX Disney Superheroes Cosplay Video

Publish date: 2024-08-26

Where do you expect the VFX effects to be implemented? Indeed, the answers might be in big projects like movies, tv-series, and other productions. But 20-year-old former Georgia State University theater major student Julian Bass took the VFX to the next level.

Julian Bass utilized the VFX for his Tik Tok video, which is kind of weird but still unique. Bass, age 20, posted a video on his Tik Tok that featured him cosplaying as Disney superheroes throughout the 20-second clip. He showcased his VFX and editing skills through the clip, which he learned all by himself.

And the 20-second-clip has led him to the pinnacle of fame and limelight; probably it sets his future career as well.

Bass started with Jedi Knight, after which he became Ben 10 and Spiderman. He also posted a video set to One Direction singer Harry Styles and Watermelon Sugar, which went viral in no time. The Tik Tok video has accumulated 5.7 million views as of now. On Twitter, it already had collected the views of more than 10 million, 300,000 retweets, and 650,000 million likes.

(©: Twitter/Julian Bass)

On the Twitter post, Bass jokingly wrote, 'if y'all can retweet this enough times that Disney calls, that'd be greatly appreciated.' However, sources have mentioned that he had not expected that it would actually happen. Talking to The Hollywood Reporter, Bass said, 'I jokingly asked for Disney and got the chairman himself.'

Bass' entertaining Tik Tok video before a long holiday in July 2020 also attained the attention of the Guardians of the Galaxy's director, James Gunn, Zach Braff, MSNBC host Joy Reid, Mark Hamill, The Lonely Island guys, and many others.

And most interestingly, one of the biggest men in Disney, Exec Bob Iger himself wrote to Bass that the world is going to know his name.

(©: Twitter/Julian Bass)

Since the video became viral, the former Georgia State University alum has been a part of several interviews of the outlets like NPR, messages from the co-president of Marvel, Louis D'Esposito, and many other opportunities.

Who Is Julian Bass?

Julian Bass is an aspiring American social media influencer. He joined Twitter in April 2020, but he already has garnered 63.8k followers on it. Similarly, he is the recipient of 839.5k followers on his Tik Tok account. 

He is a graduate of Georgia State University, where he majored in theatre. Usually, he posted several clips and entertaining videos on his Tik Tok, which he started reposting on Twitter. Nevertheless, it took him to fame.

(©: hollywoodreporter.com)

Though he started his journey on social media lately, he has already been making videos for more than nine years. He began with Lego stop-motion, and when he was at high school in suburban Atlanta, he moved to the VFX section. He also won awards at a local festival and also the National PTA Reflections award.

Later, he worked as an editor at GCPSTV when he was at Georgia State University.
