Rauw Alejandro height: How tall is Rauw Alejandro?

Publish date: 2024-08-24

Rauw Alejandro is a rising star in the world of reggaeton music, with his smooth vocals and unique style earning him a reputation as the “King of Modern Reggaeton.” 

He was born in San Juan, Puerto Rico in 1993 and was introduced to music at a young age by his father, who played guitar, and his mother, who was a backing vocalist. Growing up, he was influenced by a range of artists like Elvis Presley to Chris Brown.

Although he initially pursued soccer as a passion, he suffered an injury when he was 20 years old and turned to music as a way to lift his spirits. He began releasing songs on SoundCloud in 2014, and his debut mixtape, Punto de Equilibrio, was released in 2016. In 2018, he was chosen by Sony Music Latin to be part of “Los Próximos,” a project that sought to highlight up-and-coming talent.

Since then, Alejandro’s star has continued to rise, with collaborations with the likes of Ozuna and Nicky Jam helping to cement his reputation as a rising talent. His debut album, Afrodisíaco, was released in 2020 to critical acclaim, and his follow-up album, Vice Versa, was released in 2021. The lead single from Vice Versa, “Todo de Ti,” was a massive hit, peaking at the number 2 spot on Spotify’s Top Songs Global chart.

How tall is Rauw Alejandro?

Rauw Alejandro stands at a height of around 176 cm, which is equivalent to five feet eight inches. This puts him slightly above the average height for men in Puerto Rico, which is approximately 172 cm or five feet seven inches.

In comparison to fellow Spanish-language artist Rosalia, who stands at a height of 165 cm or five feet five inches, Rauw Alejandro is noticeably taller.
