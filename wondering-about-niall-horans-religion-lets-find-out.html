Who is Niall Horan?

Born on September 13, 1993, Niall James Horan is a celebrated Irish singer-songwriter who initially rose to fame as a member of the boy band One Direction. After the group’s hiatus, Horan ventured into a successful solo career, signing with Capitol Records. His albums “Flicker” (2017), “Heartbreak Weather” (2020), and “The Show” (2023) have garnered widespread acclaim and recognition in the music industry.
