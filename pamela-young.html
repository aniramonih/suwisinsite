Pamela Young Biography

Pamela Young is an American journalist and currently a producer and reporter at KHON 2 in Honolulu, Hawaii since February 2016 after spending over 26 years at KITV 4 (ABC Honolulu), she also worked for  KPIX and KQED in San Francisco. She is a 12 time Emmy winner and is a Peabody recipient. She is the producer and host of “Mixed Plate”, which is Hawaii’s longest-running news feature series.

She currently holds the position of NATAS VP-Hawaii and would like to continue to use her experience as a Governor.
