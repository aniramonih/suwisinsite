Video of Funny Faces swollen face in court raises concern

Publish date: 2024-08-16

A video showing Funny Face with swollen face has heavily been circulating on social media.

On Wednesday, Kumawood entertainer Lil Win along with journalist Kofi Adomaa paid Funny Face a visit in court. In the video, the comedian’s face raised concern.

Earlier in court today, Lil Win, Kofi Adomaa and some unknown persons were seen supporting Funny Face at the Kaneshie District Court in Accra.

The actor, known in private life as Nana Yaw Oduro Boateng, was charged with ‘threat of death’ and has been referred for psychiatric examination.

Funny Face was arrested on Monday by the police for threatening his baby mama, Vanessa Nicole, and other people around him.

Recall that the comedian released a shocking video over the weekend, jabbing Fada Dickson of Despite Media, Bola Ray and his destiny helper, Emmanuel Adebayor.

Right after that shocking video, he took on the mother of his three children and issued threats on her life.
