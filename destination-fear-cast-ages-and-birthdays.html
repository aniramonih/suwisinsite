Why Was Destination Fear Cancelled?

Destination Fear was cancelled because the cast members planned to start their own Youtube show. The news about the cancellation was revealed in March 2023.

All of them were heartbroken and sad as they are leaving the show they made with all their efforts and hard work.

But, the good side is this is not the end of their documentary show. They came back stronger than before.

Their brand new show Project Fear was released on June 23, 2023, on their YouTube. They announced the release date via their Instagram account.

They have even bought and owned all the equipment of Destination Fear, which includes lightning, Cameras, and other technical equipment.

The team goal is to raise $65,000 and with that money, they will give two of their viewers two hunted road trips. The series will be eight parts long.

The fans of the show blamed Zak, the Ghost Adventurers cast, and the paranormal investigator for the cancellation of Destination Fear.

Zak Bagans age is 46 years old as of 2023. He celebrate his birthday on April 5. He is also the owner of The Haunted Museum.

Zak Bagans Haunted Museum age limit is 14 to 17 years old and they must be accompanied by an adult for their safety purpose.

He was the executive producer of the Travel Channel show Destination Fear from the beginning of the show until season 4.

There are a lot of suspicions about him being the main reason the show has been canceled, as he denied to produce the show any longer.

Many allegations and comments were made on Twitter and Instagram but later, Dakota assured his fans that Zak was not the reason behind the cancellation.
