Sasha Emiri Biography

Sasha Emiri is a well-known television news producer, sports analyst, social media personality, and blogger based in St. Louis, United States. She formerly worked at KSDK as a producer and blogger. Prior to joining KSDK, she worked at KOMU-TV as a producer and reporter.

While at the University of Missouri, Sasha began her media career as a gymnastics sports analyst for Mizzou Network. During her college years, she also worked as a media official for China Open Promotions Ltd. and in social media marketing for Slim Chickens.

Sasha hosted an international gathering commemorating the tsunami in northeastern Japan in 2017. She also made a one-of-a-kind video that featured clips from the event. More than $30 million was raised for charity throughout the event. Sasha has been a Social Producer for KSDK since April 2019.

Sasha Emiri Gymnastics

Emiri’s gymnastics education was under the guidance of Enrique Trabanino and Lucas Wasson at Perfection Gymnastics. Her training also took her to various gyms, including Lynchburg Academy in Lynchburg, Virginia, and Champion Gymnastics in Louisville. Notably, she excelled as a Level 10 Region 5 competitor on three occasions.

In 2011, Emiri achieved a 23rd-place finish in the all-around at the U.S. She displayed her skills by securing a second-place finish in the all-around at the 2011 Kentucky State meet. Her prowess extended to taking first place in the all-around at the CGA Coaches Spectacular.

Emiri’s achievements included a second-place finish on the balance beam and a fourth-place finish on the vault at the 2011 Region 5 competition. Her consistency was evident with top-five finishes on both the vault and the balance beam at the 2012 Regional meet.
