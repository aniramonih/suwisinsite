Suzanne Jouannet Biography

Publish date: 2024-08-29

MARITAL STATUS
Profession Actress
French nationality

BIOGRAPHY
Trained at Cours Florent from 2016 to 2019 then a student of the Classe libre promotion XL, Suzanne Jouannet was spotted by an agent during a play. He offered him the chance to be cast for a feature film. Although she had no experience in front of the camera and had never been cast, the young woman sent a video of her attempts and landed the leading female role in Yvan Attal ‘s Choses Humaines . Taken from the eponymous novel by Karine Tuil, this drama confronts the points of view of a young man accused of rape and that of his alleged victim. For her film debut, Suzanne Jouannet distinguished herself in an ambiguous role opposite renowned actors such as Charlotte Gainsbourg , Mathieu Kassovitz and Pierre Arditi . A performance praised by the profession, which allows her to appear on the list of César Revelations 2022. Emilie Schneider
