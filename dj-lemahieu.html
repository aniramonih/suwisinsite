DJ LeMahieu | Early Life, Family, And Education

David John LeMahieu was born in Visalia, California, the United States. However, he moved to Wisconsin when he was a middle schooler.

Finally, his family settled in Michigan. His parents are Tom LeMahieu and Joan LeMahieu.

The athlete’s Father is one of the reasons why he is such a resilient baseball player. As a kid, DJ was head over heels for baseball.

Hence, Tom stepped up as a coach and began training him when he was four.

Since the days were too hot in California, the father-son duo got up early to train.

The MLB player has often described Tom as having positive energy. Moreover, he talks to his father daily, who likes imparting wise knowledge and spreading positivity.

Besides that, LeMahieu attended Brother Rice High School, where he played high school baseball. He served as a shortstop and pitcher for the Brother Rice Warriors.

During his high school career, his average was .459 with 201 hits, as a junior, he was an Alfac All-American.

Although he was selected in the 2007 MLB Draft, DJ chose to attend college.

David joined Louisiana State University to pursue collegiate baseball. Furthermore, he was an outstanding player and was a great addition to the LSU Tigers.
