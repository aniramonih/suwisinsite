Charlie McDowell Engaged To Lily Collins After Dating For A Year

Publish date: 2024-08-28

After dating for a year, Charlie McDowell is engaged to his actress girlfriend, now fiancee, Lily Collins. The 37-year-old American director proposed the love of his life to be his partner for the rest of his life by flaunting an engagement ring while they were on a romantic outing.

(Note: The couple is now married. Stay up to the latest story:)

Find the details here: Charlie McDowell And Lily Collins Are Finally Married After One Year Of Engagement

Lily Collins, age 31, announced her engagement with Charlie McDowell through an Instagram on 25 September 2020, which featured her kissing McDowell. She captioned the picture:

"I've been waiting my lifetime for you, and I can't wait to spend our lifetime together..."

Similarly, she followed with another Instagram post, where she showcased her engagement ring to her nearly 19 million followers. She captioned, 'The purest joy I've ever felt...'

Likewise, Charlie McDowell, too, posted the same picture on his Instagram feed and wrote, 'In a time of uncertainty and darkness you have illuminated my life. I will forever cherish my adventure with you.'

Both Collins and McDowell attained the congratulations messages from their friends and relatives. Ashley Tisdale, who recently announced her pregnancy, wrote, 'Congrats!!!' while Ciara penned 'Soooo happy for you! Love you, Li Li, boo! What a special day.'

The recently engaged lovebirds now look forward to planning their wedding, which is still scheduled. Congratulations, Charlie McDowell and his girlfriend-cum-fiancee, Lily Collins.

Charlie McDowell's Previous Relationships

McDowell is well-known for his relationship with the high-profile actresses, including Emilia Clarke, Hilary Duff, and Rooney Mara.

Charlie McDowell and his ex-girlfriend, Emilia Clarke (©: Pinterest)

He dated Hilary Duff for three months from September to December in 2007. Similarly, he was in a relationship with Rooney Mara for more than six years from 2010 to September 2016.

After his breakup with Mara, McDowell started dating Game Of Thrones actress Emilia Clarke in October 2018, which lasted for nearly a year until February 2019.

Charlie McDowell's Net Worth

As per tvovermind.com, McDowell is said to have an estimated net worth of $1.1 million, which he garnered as a film director. On the other hand, his recently engaged fiancee, Lily Collins, an American actress who enriches a fortune of $8 million, the data of celebritynetworth.com shows.

As a director, Charlie McDowell has directed several movies and tv shows, including Bye Bye Benjamin (2006), Silicon Valley (2016), The Discovery (2017), Dear White People (2017-18), Legion (2018), Tales from the Loop (2020), and others. Similarly, he has produced a couple of productions, including The One I Love (2014) and On Becoming a God in Central Florida (2019).

Moreover, he starred in Curb Your Enthusiasm (2004), Becker (2004), and The One I Loved (2014).

Who Is Charlie McDowell? 

Charlie McDowell is an award-winning Amerian film director and producer with some productions in his name. He was born on 10 July 1983 in Los Angeles, California, in the United States.

McDowell was born into a family of actors, where his father, Malcolm McDowell, was an actor, and his mother, Mary Steenburgen, pursued her career in acting.

During his childhood, he grew up with his four siblings, namely, Lilly, Beckett, Finnian, and Seamus.
