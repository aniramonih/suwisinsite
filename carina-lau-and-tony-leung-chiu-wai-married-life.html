Are Carina Lau and Tony Leung Chiu-wai Still Married &amp; Have a Baby?

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Chinese-born Hong Kong actress Carina Lau Kar-ling (known as Carina Lau) rose to fame from one of Hong Kong's most-watched ever series, Looking Back in Anger (1989), where she played her role as a wealthy heiress. Carina has now become a manager and business partner with her husband, Tony Leung.

However, Carina's roles as Hon Sam's wife in the two Internal Affairs sequels and an android in Wong Kar-wai's 2046 (2004) have helped her to gain international recognition. Let's take a glimpse of Carina's personal life below.

How's Carina Lau and Tony Leung's Married Life Going?

55-years-old, Carina is now living in a blissful marital relationship with her husband, Tony Leung ( Tony Leung Chiu-wai). By profession, he is an actor and singer and is considered one of Asia's most successful and internationally recognized actors. 

The couple is having a good time for thirty-one years in their blissful relationship. However, the love birds got married after dating for 19-years. They wished one another on their 13th wedding anniversary in 2021. On a similar note, actress Debbie Allen is also enjoying a happily married life with her husband of more than 35 years. 

Tony and Carina Had Their First Meeting in the 80s

Carina Lau, Hong Kong actress and Hong Kong actor Tony Leung met for the first time in 1984 when they were shooting for the movie The Replica in 1984. Five years later, they started dating in 1989.

After 19 years of relationship, Carina suggested getting married. Tony responded by saying, okay, in one condition that all the wedding arrangements should be taken care of by her. 

A Glance of Carina Lau and Tony Leung Wedding Ceremony

Carina Lau and Tony Leung, 59, tied the wedding knot on 21 July 2008 at the Uma Paro resort in Bhutan. The wedding of one of Hong Kong's biggest celebrity couples created a media frenzy in Hong Kong where companies spent hundreds of dollars to pursue the wedding party. It cost over HK$30 million (US$4 million).

The couple's wedding ceremony took place in two parts, the first being a traditional Buddhist ceremony officially recognized as a marriage in Bhutan. Similarly, for the second part, Carina showed off her 12-carat Cartier wedding ring, worth over 10 million Hong Kong dollars, as she cut her five-tiered wedding cake with Tony.

Celebrities such as Chang Chen (Zhang Zhen), Cecilia Yip (Ye Tong),and Stanley Kwan (Guan Jinpeng) attend the ceremony. In addition, singer Faye Wong (Wang Fei), former actress Brigitte Lin (Lin Qingxia), and singer Kenny Bee (Zhong Zhentao) also attended the ceremony.

Do Carina Lau and Tony Leung Have Children?

The 5 ft 5 in tall Carina Lau and Tony Leung have been in love before they were married in 2008. The couple has frequently appeared in the public eye, but they have no children so far.
