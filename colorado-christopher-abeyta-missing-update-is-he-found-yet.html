Colorado Christopher Abeyta Missing Update

It has been a staggering 37 years since the baffling disappearance of seven-month-old Christopher Abeyta from his crib in Colorado Springs.

On July 15, 1986, authorities received a distressing call reporting the missing child from the 3300 block of Ashwood Circle, just west of Quail Lake.

Christopher’s family discovered him gone when they woke up, and despite extensive efforts, he has never been seen again.

Over the years, the case has remained active, and in 2013, the family offered a $100,000 reward for any information regarding his kidnapping.

Christopher’s mother, Bernice Abeyta, tragically passed away from Cancer in 2017.

Later followed by his father, Gil, who succumbed to a heart attack in 2020.

The National Center for Missing and Exploited Children even released an age progression photo of Christopher in 2018, envisioning his appearance at 32 years old.

Despite testing DNA samples from potential matches in 2019, no conclusive evidence has emerged.

The Colorado Springs Police consider Christopher’s disappearance suspicious and urge anyone with information to contact them.

Anyone with details about him can contact at 719-444-7000 as the search for answers continues.
