Inside The Life Of Billy Stamos-The child of famous American actors John Stamos and Caitlin McHugh

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Billy Stamos is a celebrity child of famous American actors John Stamos and Caitlin McHugh. Billy was born on 10 April 2018 at 8:32 P.M. in Los Angeles. His real name is William Christopher Stamos which he was given after his late grandfather.

Billy is a lovely kid. He is adorable and affectionate to each member of his family.

Who Are Billy's Parents?

Billy's father John was born on 19 August 1963 in Cypress and his mother was born on 13 May 1986 in Altadena.

Undoubtedly Billy's parents loved him a lot and care for him. John once revealed that while his son Billy was very little, he and his wife used to check him every time to ensure that he was breathing.

However, Billy won't be the single person to get all the love from his parents. The couple John and Caitlin are reported to be trying to have baby number two.

John and Caitlin's Career

In the year 1982, John started his career after he first debuted in the TV series General Hospital. He has appeared in more than 74 TV series till now. Some of his TV series are Big Shot, Royalties, Scream Queens, Secrets of Eden, etc.

Talking about Caitlin, she began in 2007 I Am Legend. She has worked in Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, Blue Blood, The Vampire Diaries, and more.

Billy's Grandparents

Billy's grandfather, William Christopher, was a restaurateur in Gardena. He died on 6 July 2001. 

Further, Loretta Phillips was Billy's grandmother. She was a model who passed away on 22 September 2014. Timothy McHugh and Leilani McHugh are Billy's grandparents from his mother's side.

Billy's Aunts

The young kid has two aunts. They are Janeen Stamos and Alaina Stamos and are the biological siblings of John.

Janeen and Alaina both are teachers by profession. They are very close to their brother and his son.
