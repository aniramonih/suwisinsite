Kailyn Lowry's Ex-BFF Lawyers Up To Protect Himself From Her Anger

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Kailyn Lowry has not been having an easy time lately.

Last week, she revealed that she had been cut from the Teen Mom 2 episode that was set to air. This led to a lot of drama that featured Briana DeJesus and Ashley Jones. Names were called and things were said, but Kailyn ultimately tried to keep out of it.

But now, she’s being called a few more names, by someone who used to be her best friend! They had no kind words to say about Kailyn in their latest spill… and now they’re lawyering up to protect themselves!
