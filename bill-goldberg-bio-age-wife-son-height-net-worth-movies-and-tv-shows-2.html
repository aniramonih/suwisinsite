Who is Bill Goldberg? Bill Goldberg Biography and Wiki

Bill Goldberg is an American actor, professional wrestler, and retired football player who was born and raised in Tulsa, Oklahoma, United States of America. He is well known for his time in World Championship Wrestling (WCW) and is currently signed to WWE as a part-time wrestler. As an actor, he is known for appearing in movies and TV shows including The Goldbergs, The Longest Yard, WCW Monday Netro among others listed herein.
