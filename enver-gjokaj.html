Enver Gjokaj Biography

Enver Gjokaj is an American actor who is famous for his roles in Dollhouse, a science fiction TV series as Victor, and in Agent Carter as Agent Daniel Sousa. His films credits include The Express(2008) as Dave Sarette alongside Dennis Quaid, Taking Chance (2009) as Corporal Arenz, and Eagle Eye(2008) as Remote Pilot.

Gjokaj other well-known roles include The Avengers(2012) as Young Cop, The Rookie (2019-2020) as Donovan, and Emergence (2019) as Agent Ryan Brooks.

Enver Gjokaj Education

Enver studied at Amador High School, in Sutter Creek, California, where he was very active in plays and shows. Gjokaj graduated from the University of California, Berkeley with a Bachelor of Arts in English in 2002.

He later joined New York University’s Graduate Acting Program at the Tisch School of the Arts, where he graduated with a Master of Fine Arts. He continued participating in films and was a semi-finalist for Tisch’s Vilar Global Fellows Program, a Rhodes Scholar–style program in the Arts at New York University.
