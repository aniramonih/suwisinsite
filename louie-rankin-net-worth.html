What is Louie Rankin's Net Worth?

Louie Rankin was a Jamaican dancehall reggae artist and actor who had a net worth of $400 thousand at the time of his death in September 2019. Louie Rankin was born in St. Thomas, Jamaica. As a reggae artist his most successful single was "Typewriter" in 1992. As an actor Rankin first starred as Lennox in the movie Belly in 1998.

He starred as Teddy Bruck Shut in the film Shottas in 2002 and as Choppa in the movie We Run These Streets in 2014. In 2015 Rankin starred as Leroy Lucky Johnson and associate produced the film Rainbow City. He also starred as Radigan in the 2015 short Q. Unfortunately Louie died on September 30, 2019 in a car crash in Canada.
