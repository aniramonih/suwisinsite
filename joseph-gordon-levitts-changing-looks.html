Joseph Gordon-Levitt's Changing Looks

Publish date: 2024-08-20

See his transformation through the years.

1995

The baby-faced 14-year-old guest-starred on Roseanne.

1999

Now almost 18, the Third Rock from the Sun star sported a very '90s ear-length crop.

2004

A dapper Gordon-Levitt made his adult debut in director Gregg Araki's Mysterious Skin.

2006

A buzz cut for the premiere of Shadowboxer.

2008

For the Young Hollywood Awards, the Stop-Loss star channeled James Dean with a pomaded style.

2010

He showed off a little scruff for the L.A. debut of Inception.

2011

The 50/50 lead put in an appearance at the Hollywood Film Awards wearing a classic pompadour.

2012

Gordon-Levitt looked just plain handsome at the premiere of The Dark Knight Rises.

2013

The actor showed off his charming good looks at the Toronto International Film Festival to debut his film Don Jon.

2014

The actor changes up his look with a pair of glasses.

2015

Gordon-Levitt looked adorable with a shorter cut at opening night of the 53rd New York Film Festival.

2016

The actor showcased his signature smirk while promoting his film Snowden.
