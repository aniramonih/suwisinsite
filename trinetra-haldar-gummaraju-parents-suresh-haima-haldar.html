Trinetra Haldar Gummaraju, previously known as Angad Gummaraju, was born on June 17, 1997, in Manipal, Karnataka. She is an Indian LGBTQ+ advocate, medical intern, actress, and content creator.

Trinetra gained prominence by openly discussing transgender rights, feminism, and more on her Instagram platform.

Through her journey, documented on Instagram and YouTube, she became one of India’s first transgender content creators.

A doctor and a Forbes India 30 Under 30 awardee, Trinetra leverages her influence to raise awareness about gender, sexuality, mental health, and the challenges faced by the LGBTQIA+ community.

Her advocacy sheds light on crucial issues like queerphobia, bullying, and intersectionality, making her a prominent figure in these spaces.
