Matt Steffanina Net Worth – $7 million

What is Matt Steffanina’s net worth? Is Matt Steffanina married?

Introduction

Matt Steffanina is an American YouTuber, dancer, and choreographer who won The Amazing Race 28.

Biography

He was born on October 20, 1986, in Wintergreen, Virginia, USA.

During his youth, Steffanina participated in many snowboarding competitions.

At age 18, Matt began to teach himself how to dance.

He started to work as the head choreographer of the University of Virginia hip-hop dance crew after winning a dance competition.

Steffanina moved to Los Angeles in 2010.

YouTube

On September 1, 2011, Steffanina created his self-entitled YouTube channel. It has over 12.8 million subscribers.

In July 2012, Matt started the YouTube channel ”Dance Tutorials LIVE.” As of December 2020, the channel has more than 3.08 million subscribers.

Matt typically uploads videos of hip-hop dance lessons.

”I’ve realized the most important thing is maintaining your passion for what you’re doing,” Steffanina said in an interview. He added: ”If it starts to be about the views, you will fall out of love with dance.”

The Amazing Race

In 2016, he was one of the contestants in the 28th season of ”The Amazing Race.” The show was created by Bertram van Munster and Elise Doganieri. 

Matt won the competition with Dana Alexa.

© Guliver / Getty Images

In 2018, he won a Streamy Award in the ‘Dance’ category. Also in 2018, Matt was a judge in the dance competition ”Dance USA Dance” and appeared in the documentary ”Building a Dancer.”

In November 2018, Steffanina inked a deal with Abrams Artists Agency for representation.

In 2019, he served as the host of ”Step By Step With Matt Steffanina.”

Wife/Girlfriend

For a while, Matt Steffanina was engaged to Dana Alexa, a professional dancer and choreographer who has an Instagram account with more than 439k followers and a YouTube channel named ”DanaAlexaDance,” with over 429k subscribers.

© Guliver / Getty Images

She also performed on The Real Housewives of New Jersey and Bravo’s Watch What Happens Live.

Together, they won The Amazing Race 28.

Matt and Dana first met at the shooting of a music video called ”Ashes.”

They were about to get married. In April 2013, Matt responded to a fan: ”getting married next October.”

”I think for a honeymoon, we will choose something a little more relaxing,” Steffanina told Hello Magazine in March 2016. ”Maybe a beautiful beach with some downtime would be a nice change from the craziness of racing.”

After Steffanina and Dana broke up, he started to date Destiny Kaye.

In 2023, Matt Steffanina began a relationship with Ann Mcferran. She’s the founder of Glamnetic.

Ann Mcferran and Matt Steffanina at The 2023 Streamy Awards – @Getty

At the time of writing, Matt Steffanina is not married.
