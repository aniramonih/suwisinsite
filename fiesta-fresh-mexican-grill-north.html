Fiesta Fresh Mexican Grill-North | Hilton Head Island

Publish date: 2024-08-09

Hilton Head Island & Bluffton’s fast-causal Mexican restaurants featuring fresh Mexican food and attentive customer service, in a clean and friendly atmosphere. Influenced by the Jalisco region of Mexico, our menu is made up of several traditional Mexican dishes with many unique flavors prepared from scratch daily using the highest quality ingredients.

As a family run restaurant our staff takes great pride in the quality and freshness of the food we serve, from our homemade tacos shells, fresh daily rice, salsas, pico de gallo and guacamole, to the use of all white meat chicken and shoulder tender steak.
