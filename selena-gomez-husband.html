What is Benny Blanco’s Profession?

The real name of Benny Blanco is Benjamin Joseph Levin and he is an American record producer. The 2013 Hal David Starlight Award from the Songwriters Hall of Fame goes to him.

In addition, he was the 2017 iHeartRadio Producer of the Year Award winner and the recipient of five nonconsecutive BMI Songwriter of the Year Awards. Levin co-wrote and co-produced many popular tracks in the late 2000s and early 2000s while learning from him.

Thanks to Levin’s efforts, artists such as Ed Sheeran, Justin Bieber, Halsey, Katy Perry, Maroon 5, Christina Aguilera, Kesha, Britney Spears, Rihanna, Sia, The Weeknd, Kanye West, Avicii, Charlie Puth, Keith Urban, OneRepublic, Wiz Khalifa, J Balvin, Kali Uchis, Juice Wrld and SZA have sold over 500 million album-equivalent units worldwide.

Who Has Selena Gomez Dated in the Past?

Selena Gomez dated many persons in the past and below we provided some information related to her dating history

Selena and Nick dated in 2008 when they were both Disney Channel stars. She also starred in the Jonas Brothers’ 2008 music video for Burnin’ Up. It is not yet officially confirmed when the couple split up.

They had a relationship in 2009 before he dated Selena’s best friend “Taylor Swift”. Selena Gomez disclosed in 2009 that she got to know Taylor Lautner through Kristen Stewart, his Twilight co-star. She said at the time:
