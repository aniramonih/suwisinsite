Arnelle Simpson is famous as the oldest child and daughter of O.J. Simpson. Her father, O.J. Simpson, is an American former football running back, actor, and broadcaster. He played in the National Football League (NFL) primarily with the Buffalo Bills and is arguably one of the greatest running backs ever. 

Once a popular figure with the American public, O.J. ‘s professional success became overshadowed by his trial and controversial acquittal for the murders of his former wife, Nicole Brown, and her friend, Ron Goldman.

Consequently, O.J. Simpson’s daughter, Arnelle, became famous following her involvement in her father’s murder trial. In addition, she was at the receiving end of persistent scrutiny by the media.

However, the former footballer’s eldest child opted to live a quiet life. Due to her father’s notorious run-ins with the law, Arnelle Simpson has been a constant source of interest to the public. Here’s a glimpse into her life.

Who Is Arnelle Simpson?

While testifying during her father’s infamous murder trial, Arnelle told the jury that she was born in December 1968, the same day her father won the Heisman trophy. As earlier stated, she is the first child of the former football player and his first wife, Marguerite L. Whitley. Despite being a subject of interest to eagle-eyed fans, Arnelle Simpson keeps most details about her early life private. 

However, speaking about her childhood experience in an interview with Georgia Newsday in 2014, she said, “Back then, we were the first black family in that neighborhood in Brentwood.” Consequently, this proves that O.J. Simpson’s daughter, Arnelle, grew up in a lovely neighborhood with her parents and had a good education.

Brown’s passing marked the start of a difficult period for the family as O.J., alleged to be the killer, became the center of public backlash. Arnelle, who had lived through the best days of their family, perhaps felt the change more than her siblings.

As the eldest child, Arnelle Simpson bore the brunt of her family’s troubles. However, she couldn’t secure a stable job due to her father’s arrest. Arnelle struggled to carve out a career and had a few significant employment opportunities, including working for a rapper called Hash. In addition, reports suggest she has produced several fashion shows.

Moreover, it became public knowledge that Arnelle had an alcohol problem, which contributed to the exuberant spending of her father’s money. In addition, reports also suggest that O.J. knew about his daughter’s alcohol problem, as it had been going on even before he went to prison.
