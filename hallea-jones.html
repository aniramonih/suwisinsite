Hallea Jones (born April 07, 1997) is a Canadian Actress, Model, Musician, Singer, Songwriter, and Social Media Personality from Toronto, Ontario. She received her career’s breakthrough role with a Netflix series “Locke & Key”.

It is an American supernatural horror/drama web television series. Furthermore, Joe Hill (American author and comic book writer) created the program. In fact, Locke & Key is based on the comic book series of the same name by Joe.

Jones played one of the supporting roles as Eden Hawkins in the show. Her Netflix show was released on the platform on February 07, 2020. The story revolves around 3 siblings who move to their home in Massachusetts after their father was brutally murdered.

Later, they discovered a set of magical keys that has various powers. But little do they know, an insidious devil also wants the keys. The music was given by Torin Borrowdale (American music composer).

Acting Career, Locke & Key Netflix Shows & Movies

The 22-years-old actress started her career after signing with a talent agency named “Fountainhead Talent” in 2017. Hallea began to coach under Robyn Friedman. Later, she started to work in various TV series for minor roles.

As per the source, the young artist also signed a movie through her agency but there are no details about it on the internet as of February 2020. Jones received an opportunity to work in “Locke & Key” after giving an audition via Fountainhead Talent.
