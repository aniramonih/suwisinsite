Is Bernard Hill in Game of Thrones?

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Bernard Hill is an actor from England. He gained notoriety for his roles as Captain Edward Smith in Titanic, King Théoden in the Lord of the Rings trilogy, and Luther Plunkitt, the San Quentin Prison Warden in Clint Eastwood’s True Crime.

Is Bernard Hill in Game of Thrones?

Bernard Hill is in Game of Thrones. In the third season, a supporting role is played by Lord Terrence Tymber, also known as “Terror Terrence.” He makes his acting debut in “Valar Dohaeris,” played by guest star Bernard Hill.

Bernard Hill starred as Police Constable Cluff in the 1976 episode of Crown Court on Granada Television, “The Jolly Swagmen.”

Bernard Hill received a nomination for his portrayal of David Blunkett in the one-off satirical drama A Very Social Secretary at the 2006 BAFTA TV Awards and International Emmy Award.

In the six-part BBC version of Wolf Hall from 2015, Hill plays Thomas Howard, 3rd Duke of Norfolk.
