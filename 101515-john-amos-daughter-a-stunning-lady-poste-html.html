John Amos' daughter looked stunning in sweet photo with her famous dad

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Amos is known for his work on the ‘70s and ‘80s shows like the "Mary Tyler Moore Show" and "Good Times." His daughter, Shannon, is a grown-up woman who looks a lot like her famous dad.

John Allen Amos Jr. is an American actor who is best known for his role as James Evans Sr. on the CBS show "Good Times." However, his resume includes other works such as "Roots," "Coming to America," "The West Wing," "Two and a Half Men," and "The Ranch."

The actor, a family man, shared a selfie with his daughter, Shannon, while they were celebrating Thanksgiving together a few years ago.

He captioned the picture with:

"She will always be my beautiful baby girl. Me and my lovely daughter Shannon Amos on Thanksgiving!"

Shannon, who is the eldest daughter of Amos with ex-wife Noel Mickelson, looks quite happy posing next to her father. The gorgeous woman is a successful writer/producer and founder of Afterglow Multimedia. She has produced music videos for artists like Snoop Dogg, Jodeci, Ice Cube and Dr. Dre.

She has also worked in the production of Warner Bros. films such as "Blood Diamond," "Letters from Iwo Jima," and "Good Night and Good Luck." In 2009, she launched Afterglow Multimedia, LLC, a boutique talent management and production company that handles the career of thousands of YouTubers and creates media content for diverse platforms.

Wellness Travel Mama is her side activity. A lifestyle blog where Shannon shares with fans everything about her travels around the world and what she learns from every new culture she visits.

John Amos, on the other hand, is now the animator of a YouTube Kids animated series title "Nubbin’ & Friends" and he recently went to Atlanta to talk about it. He also talked about wanting to animate his recent children's book "A World without Color."

The animated series "Nubbin & Friends" is a mixture of shows like "Sesame Street" and "Dora the Explorer" and features stories that help kids learn lessons like the alphabet and math.
