Normal People Wiki

Normal People is a romantic psychological drama limited series co-produced by Element Pictures for BBC Three and Hulu in association with Screen Ireland, based on Sally Rooney's 2018 novel of the same name. The show depicts the relationship between Marianne Sheridan and Connell Waldron as they go through adolescence and adulthood, from secondary school to their undergraduate years in Trinity College.

The series was directed by Lenny Abrahamson and Hettie Macdonald and primarily written by Sally Rooney and Alice Birch. Normal People premiered on BBC Three in the UK on April 26, 2020, followed by weekly airings on BBC One, and on RTÉ One in Ireland on April 28, 2020. In the US, the series was released in its entirety on Hulu on April 29, 2020.

It has received critical acclaim for its performances, directing, writing, aesthetics, and portrayal of mature content. The series earned four nominations at the 72nd Primetime Emmy Awards, including Outstanding Lead Actor for Paul Mescal and Outstanding Directing for Lenny Abrahamson.

Normal People Plot
