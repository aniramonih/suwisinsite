WWE Confirms Kevin Owens Injury from 12/1 Friday Night SmackDown

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Kevin Owens puts his all into every performance. Sadly, it seems he went over the line a bit, and now he has an injury to deal with.

Kevin Owens incurred an injury on Friday Night SmackDown. WWE dropped an injury update regarding Kevin Owens following his appearance on the December 1st episode of Friday Night SmackDown.

WWE has disclosed that Kevin Owens sustained a fracture in his 4th and 5th metacarpal bones. This revelation came after Owens underwent an X-ray examination following the December 1 episode of WWE SmackDown. Initially, there was uncertainty regarding the severity of Owens’ condition.

UPDATE: @fightowensfight has sustained an injury, fracturing his 4th and 5th metacarpal during #SmackDown last night.

Speculation had arisen after his match against Grayson Waller that Owens may have suffered a broken hand. During the bout, Waller had specifically targeted KO’s hand.

It was not said that Kevin Owens is out of the WWE United States Title tournament. So, he doesn’t seem to be missing any time. He will just have to go a bit easier, but that will also be hard for him.
