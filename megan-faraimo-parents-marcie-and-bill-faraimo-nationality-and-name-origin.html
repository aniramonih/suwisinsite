Megan Faraimo’s grandparents were from American Samoa, despite the fact that both of her parents were born in the United States. Continue reading to know more about the 21-year-old athlete representing the AAPI community.

Megan Faraimo Parents Marcie and Bill Faraimo Origin

Megan Faraimo’s father’s parents are originally from American Samoa, an island in Oceania before they relocated to California. Megan Faraimo is one of the few softball players representing Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders (AAPI) in sport. 

Marcie once tweeted during AAPI Heritage Month that she hopes she is representing her community and family in a way that her father and family would be proud of.

Megan’s father, Bill, was one of seven siblings, and according to Megan, all of them were athletes. In high school, her father was a quarterback, and her mother was a softball and volleyball player.

Because Megan was born into an athletic family, athletics came naturally to her.

Insight On Megan Faraimo Nationality And Ethnicity

Megan Faraimo was born in California and hence holds American nationality.

She is classified as Asian-American because the US Census only recognizes six ethnic groups. However, she belongs to the ethnic group Samoans.

Her grandparents on her father’s side immigrated to California from American Samoa.

Megan Faraimo Age And Height Disclosed

Megan Faraimo, age 21, was born on July 14, 2000, in San Diego, California, to parents Marcie and Bill Faraimo. She is 6 feet tall, which is not surprising given that her father is 6’2″ and her mother is 6’1″ tall.

In 2020, she was named Softball America Pitcher of the Year as a sophomore at UCLA. 

She wanted to major in Political Science and minor in African-American studies when she was 17. At the time, she wanted to pursue a career in law.

Megan Faraimo Siblings – Who Are They?

Megan Faraimo is the second child of parents Marcie and Bill Faraimo and has three siblings.

Muta is her younger sister, and Matthew and Madden are her older and younger brothers, respectively. Matthew is older than her by three years and Madden is five years her junior.

From 2017 to 2020, Matthew played men’s volleyball for the University of Southern California Trojans.

Madden Faraimo is a 6’3”, 210-pound safety who has yet to be recruited by a college, despite his interest in San Diego State and USC. He played Little League baseball when he was 12 years old.
