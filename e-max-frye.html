E. Max Frye is an American film director and screenwriter. He was born in 1956 in Oregon, United States. After graduating, he moved to Los Angeles and began working as a screenwriter. He wrote the screenplay for the 1988 film, "The Accidental Tourist," which was nominated for an Academy Award.
