Philmar Alipayo Wiki, Age (Andi Eigenmanns Husband) Biography, Family, Facts

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Philmar Alipayo (born in 1992) is a Filipino Surfer, Public Figure, Famous Personality, and Celebrity Partner from Siargao. He is well recognized as Andi Eigenmann’s spouse (Filipino actress and model).

She started her career in 2007 in a soap opera “Prinsesa ng Banyera” as Sandy. Later, she participated in the reality show “Pinoy Big Brother: Teen Edition Plus”. Andi then became one of the 12 finalists of the MYX VJ Search 2009.

Her career’s breakthrough role came with a 2010 drama series, Agua Bendita. The actress’s performance where she played a dual role Agua and Bendita appreciated by fans a lot.

Some of her other popular projects “Mamarazzi”, “Your Song”, “Snake Rattle and Roll 12”, “Kahit Puso’y Masugatan”, “Pridyider”, “A Secret Affair”, “Galema: Anak ni Zuma”, “Maalaala Mo Kaya”, and much more.

Marriage with Wife Andi Eigenmann & Children

Philmar Alipayo tied the knot with his wife, Andrea Nicole “Andi” Guck Eigenmann, a few years back. However, there are no much details about their love life, marriage date, and venue on the internet.

On July 23, 2019, the couple welcomed their child, a daughter named Keliana Alohi “Lilo” Eigenmann Alipayo. Earlier, the actress gave birth to her first kid named Adrianna Gabrielle “Ellie” Eigenmann on 23 November 2011.

However, there were many rumors surrounded on the internet regarding Ellie’s biological father. During an interview, Andi Eigenmann told the audience that Jake Ejercito (actor) is the dad of her girl. Jake is well recognized as the son of Joseph Estrada (former Philippine President).

She said that Jake is with her and Keliana Alohi since her birth. Therefore, it doesn’t matter if he is the biological parent or not because the model considered him as a father figure to Ellie.

There were also rumors in 2011 that Andrea Nicole’s ex-boyfriend could be the father of her first child. In September 2016, Andi’s sister confirmed in a podcast that Jake Ejercito was the biological daddy of Ellie as a paternity test was taken.
